Explain why there does not exist a list of six polynomials that are linearly independent in P4(F)\mathcal P_4(\mathbf F)P4​(F).

The list (1,x,…,x4)(1,x,\dots,x^4)(1,x,…,x4) in P4(F)\mathcal P_4(\mathbf F)P4​(F) is spanning, by 2.23 the length of any linearly independent list ≤\le≤ length of any spanning list.
