$$
\Gamma(S) = S \circ \pi.
$$
1. Show that $\Gamma$ is a linear map.
2. Show that $\Gamma$ is injective
3. Show that $\text{range }\Gamma = \{T \in \mathcal L(V,W) : Tu = 0 \text{ for every }u\in U\}$
